For this image as part of my glitch art project, I decided to glitch a picture I took during my first time riding on a bamboo raft in china. I decided to glitch my picture in a way where it transforms from a vivid memory to a very dark and fuzzy one. I decided to go with this transition because when I look at this picture, I remember that good memories fade away. I don't always remember good memories unless I get a visual refreshent of them. It gives off the feeling of how fun does not always last forever and that life will continue after fun.
